Security Tips

Best Practices

USE A PASSWORD MANAGER Store unique passwords for every account. Never reuse passwords across services.
ENABLE 2FA EVERYWHERE Two-factor authentication adds a critical second layer even if your password is leaked.
LONGER = STRONGER A 20-character passphrase beats a complex 8-character password by billions of iterations.
AVOID PERSONAL INFO Names, birthdays, and keyboard patterns are the first things attackers try.
